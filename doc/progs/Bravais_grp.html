Program Bravais_grp

Usage

Bravais_grp 'file'
file: bravais_TYP containing the finite unimodular group G.

Description

Calculates generators of the Bravais group B(G) of G. If the space of G-invariant quadratic forms is given in 'file', it relies on its correctness and echoes it in the output. Otherwise it is calculated as well.

Remarks

See also for Aut_grp (for forms without a group in file).

Examples

  1. Do two given groups have Z-equivalent copies which lie in a finite unimodular group?
