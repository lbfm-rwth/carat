Program Form_elt

Usage

Form_elt 'file' [-i] [-f]
file: bravais_TYP containing the finite unimodular group G.

Description

Calculates the elementatry divisors of the trace bifo of the group G given in file, i.e. takes a Z-basis B (resp. B') of the invariant forms of G (respectively Gtr), and gives the Smith normal form of Trace(Bi * B'j)i,j.

Options

-i  : Use the function invar_space to calculate the space of invarinat forms.
-f  : Force the formspace of G to be computed again, even if it is given.
